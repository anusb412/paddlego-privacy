Paddle-Go Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

Paddle-Go ("we", "our", or "us") operates this mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Email address and optional username when creating an account.
  • Location Data: With permission, we access approximate location to provide nearby paddle spots and weather updates. Location data is not retained longer than necessary.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, device type, operating system, IP address, and session activity.
  • User-Generated Content: Forum posts, profile pictures, and uploaded images stored securely through our backend provider.
  • Advertising Data: Advertising identifiers and device information may be collected when ads are displayed.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality.
  • To personalize nearby recommendations and weather.
  • To improve performance and fix bugs.
  • To communicate regarding support or security issues.
  • To display advertisements.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Backend Services: We use Appwrite for authentication, database storage, and file storage (including user-uploaded images).
  • Advertising Partners: We may use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect advertising identifiers and usage data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Compliance: Information may be disclosed if required by law.
  • Public Content: Forum posts and profile content are visible to other users.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure storage practices through our service providers. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access to your personal data.
  • You may request correction of inaccurate information.
  • You may request deletion of your account (see Account Deletion section).
  • You may opt out of location access or personalized advertising through your device settings.

7. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion by emailing help.paddlego@outlook.com from their registered email address.

Upon verification, we will permanently delete the account and associated personal data within 30 days, unless retention is legally required.

8. Children's Privacy

Paddle-Go is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
help.paddlego@outlook.com
